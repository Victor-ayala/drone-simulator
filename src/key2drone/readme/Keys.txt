Keyboard Controller commands

--- Bebop General ---
T			Bebop Takeoff
L			Bebop Land
H			Bebop Hover


--- Bebop movement---
W-A-S-D 		Bebop Linear Movement (forward-left-backward-right)
Q-E			Bebop Angular Movement (left-right)
G-B			Bebop Vertical Movement (up-down)
Up-arrow		Increase vertical speed
Down-arrow		Decrease vertical speed
Right-arrow		Increase linear speed
Left-arrow		Decrease linear speed


--- Camera control --- (not working in Sim)
I-K			Camera Tilt (up-down)
J-L			Camera Pan (left-right)
O			Camera Reset Position


--- General ---
ESC			Exit Keyboard Controller
